Movie Recommendation System

WHAT IS OUR TEAM NAME?

What is the problem?

There's nothing more frustrating to someone than having free time but not knowing what to watch or just being tired of rewatching the same movie over again and again. So, in order to lower your stress, our movie recommendation service will recommend the perfect movies that you would have never thought of so you can sit back and relax.

How does it work?

In order to get the most accurate understanding of what movies that you might be interested in, we decided that it was best to allow the user to only select the movies that they strongly remember or like rather than all the movies they have seen. By doing this we can both limit the number of entries that goes into the system and offer a more accurate result to the user. After collecting a series of movies that the user has already seen, this data is sent to the system where it extracts the attributes of the movies, namely: title name, genre, cast, and the plot. Using this it compares the attributes of the movies selected by the user to the movies in our database of over 100,000 movies using cosine similarity and selects the top 8 movies to recommend to the user.
